Learn How to Make a Branch Link!




Do you want the app to open to a the homepage or to a specific page?

  • If no deep linking is desired, you do not need to add any parameters and can leave the link data blank.

  • To open a specific page of your app, you'll need to have a deep link routing structure in place and add those parameters to your link. Apps use Key and Value pairs to tell the app what to do when opened. Common routing structures include canonical_url, deeplink_path, or simple keys such as page=account_summary.
